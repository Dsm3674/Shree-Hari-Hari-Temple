SevaAndInspire (SevaSAI) - Non-Religious PROGRAM(s)

Mission:
SevaSAI (SevaAndInspire) Programs embodies the mission of Seva (volunteer and assist) And Inspiration (Spoorti) – Programs are geared towards community engagement, involvement, inclusion and diversity. Program aspires to engage with community, help other charitable/humanitarian causes, and developing/encouraging cultural inclusion and diversity- a secular program offered to eligible participants/organizations without regard to their faith or willingness to participate in religious activities.

Programs:

1. Canned-food drives:
Food/canned-food and supplies to supply and donate to local Soup kitchens and food banks/pantries. Run semi-annual food collection drives. Gather canned food, or groceries for the event. Supply the collected and purchased items to local Food pantries and Food-Banks. Special Drives to be organized towards COVID-19 and other critical disasters and causes.

2. Blankets and Backpacks:
Blankets and Backpack supplies to supply and donate to local homeless and other sheltering organizations. Run annual winter Blanket/Backpack/winter-supply collection drives. Gather Blanket/Backpack/winter-supply collection drives. Supply the collected and purchased items to local Homeless and other Sheltering organizations.

3. Cultural Events and Programs:
Organize cultural events around major holidays, including but not limited to – Bharata Natyam, Kuchipudi Dance. Conduct cultural events, including Bharata Natyam, Kuchipudi dance in collaboration with local cultural and diversity orgs. Encourage and Conduct Youth singers of diverse and ethnic cultural heritage and languages.

4. Donations to Other Charitable Orgs:
Organize Fundraising and contribution program to other non-religious/non-political humanitarian causes and organizations.

Previous Completed Programs:
• Canned food drive at NTFB (North Texas Food Bank)
• Blankets supply to Dallas Downtown homeless people
• Breakfast and Lunch support to Austin Street Center (Dallas)

Upcoming/Planned Programs:
• Yoga - teaching and practicing for achieving better physical and mental health
• Conduct classes to teach different languages such as - Hindi, Telugu and Tamil
• Semi-annual food drive at NTFB (North Texas Food Bank)
• Blankets supply to Dallas Downtown homeless people
• School supplies for under privileged kids
• Breakfast and Lunch support to Austin Street Center (Dallas) - OnHold due to COVID-19
• Unconditional support to frontline warriors

Important Notes:
* SevaSAI program is for charity, food-drives, contributions/donations to local and humanitarian needs utilizes multiple volunteer drives which require thousands of dollars each year. SevaSAI maintains its own budget for its programs, fundraising, donations, and its expenses.
