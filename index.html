o achieve nirvana

 nirvana, it

achieve enlightenment that is the reason
 why Sri Adi Sankara Charya mentioned as “Gnanaa

 minds and bodies. If we need

nirvana. A human  being has 3 kinds of needs that 
need to be fulfilled and that is the reason
 they incarnate as a human beings.
The needs while they are alive. The needs when they are in heaven.

 Sivasya Hrudayam Vishnuhu|
